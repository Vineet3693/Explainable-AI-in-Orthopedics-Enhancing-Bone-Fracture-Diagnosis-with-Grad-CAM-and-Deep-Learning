SLIDE 11: CORE FEATURES - EXPLAINABILITY
================================================================================

Title: Explainable AI - Building Trust

Why Explainability Matters:
  • Clinical trust and adoption
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Error detection
  • Educational value

Three Complementary Methods:

1. Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping)
   • Visual heatmaps
   • Shows "where the model looks"
   • Highlights fracture regions
   • Most intuitive for clinicians

2. Integrated Gradients
   • Attribution-based
   • Pixel-level importance
   • Mathematically rigorous
   • Complements Grad-CAM

3. LIME (Local Interpretable Model-agnostic Explanations)
   • Local approximations
   • Feature importance
   • Model-agnostic
   • Additional validation

Output:
  → Color-coded heatmaps overlaid on X-ray
  → Red/yellow = High attention (likely fracture)
  → Blue/green = Low attention
